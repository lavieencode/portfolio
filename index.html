"La Vie en Code" Blog

Dedicated to the Self-Educated

La Vie en Code is my pride and joy. Since embarking on this ridiculous endeavor to transition into web development fully back in May 2015, I had wanted to write about my experiences. I feel strongly that women--and particularly, minority women--are often afraid to take the initial steps toward web dev, despite the fact that they may be extremely interested, or even have some skills under their belt already.

I am out to eliminate this fear.

When is the last time you saw code anthropomorphized as a speed dating partner? How about a genuine story of a woman's first hackathon? You'll find it here: the self-deprecation over my own code being weird, the resulting pick-me-up talk to myself about not comparing myself to other developers... the laughter, and the tears.

Are you ready to come read now?
